Video Upload Workflow

This tool helps you process and upload videos to YouTube with the following steps:

  1. Color Edit: Applies a color editing filter to your video
  2. Transcription: Transcribes the video using OpenAI's Whisper
  3. Chapter Generation: Generates chapters and suggested titles
  4. Title Selection: Lets you choose a title from suggestions or create your own
  5. Description Editing: Allows you to edit the description with chapter markers
  6. Final Review: Preview everything before upload
  7. Upload: Uploads the video to YouTube with all metadata
